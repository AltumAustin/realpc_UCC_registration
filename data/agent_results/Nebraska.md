## Nebraska UCC Searcher/Filing Access Requirements - Research Findings

### 1. Filing Office
**Nebraska Secretary of State - Business Services Division**

**Confidence Level: HIGH ✓**

- Primary filing office for UCC financing statements in Nebraska
- Exception: Real property-related collateral (as-extracted collateral, timber, or fixtures) may have different filing requirements

### 2. Authorization Model
**Account-Based System (As of December 2025)**

**Confidence Level: HIGH ✓**

- **New System Launch:** December 8, 2025 - making this current for 2026
- **Account Requirement:** Users must create a login at https://business.nebraska.gov to file most online requests and filings
- **Major Change:** The previous $100 yearly subscription with Nebraska.gov (Tyler Technology) is **NO LONGER REQUIRED** as of the new system
- **Optional Enhancement:** Frequent Payor Account available (allows pre-depositing funds for future filings)

### 3. Requirements

**Confidence Level: MEDIUM-HIGH**

**For Filing:**
- Account creation at https://business.nebraska.gov (required)
- Debtor authorization per Nebraska UCC 9-509 (debtor must authorize filing via signed record or security agreement)
- Electronic Filing Services Agreement submission to Secretary of State (based on older system - status unclear if still required under new system launched Dec 2025)

**For Searching:**
- Online searches available at https://business.nebraska.gov
- Document number searches currently available free of charge
- No explicit authorization requirements mentioned for conducting searches

### 4. Access Method
**Online Portal**

**Confidence Level: HIGH ✓**

- **Primary Portal:** https://business.nebraska.gov
- **Services Available:**
  - UCC filings
  - EFS (Electronic Filing Services)
  - XML filings
  - Searches
  - Document image services
- **Payment Methods:** Credit card, ACH, or Frequent Payor Account (paid at time of submission)
- **No More Monthly Billing:** All fees paid at time of submission

### 5. Fee Schedule

**Confidence Level: HIGH ✓**

**Search Fees:**
- Written request (mail/paper): $5.00 per debtor name
- Online request: $4.50 per debtor name
- Document number searches: **Currently FREE**

**Filing Fees:**
- Online filing: $11.00 (for UCC financing statements, continuations, statutory liens)
- In-house/Paper filing: $14.00
- UCC Terminations: **NO FEE**
- Filing fees cover documents regardless of number of debtor indexes/addresses or attachments

**Note:** Nebraska statute §9-525 establishes these fee structures

### Key Findings Summary

1. **Modernized System:** Nebraska recently launched a new UCC/EFS system (December 8, 2025) that eliminates the previous $100 annual subscription requirement
2. **Simplified Access:** Account creation is now the primary requirement rather than paid subscriptions
3. **Competitive Pricing:** Online filing fees ($11) are lower than paper filings ($14), with free document number searches
4. **Multiple Payment Options:** Credit card, ACH, or pre-funded Frequent Payor Account
5. **Comprehensive Online Services:** All major UCC functions available through the business.nebraska.gov portal
