### 1. WEST VIRGINIA

**Filing Office:** West Virginia Secretary of State, UCC Division

**Authorization Model:** **Open Access** (No formal authorized searcher program)
- Confidence Level: High
- Any user can access the UCC online system directly
- No certification or special authorization required
- All filings must be done electronically since September 15, 2016 (no paper filings accepted)

**Key Fees:**
- UCC1 Initial Filing: $20.00 (online)
- UCC3 Amendment/Other Records: $20.00
- Information Request/Search Certificate: $10.00 (written request)
- Non-certified searches: Available online directly
- Certified searches: Available for purchase through online system (since February 27, 2018, manual searches discontinued)

**Access Method:**
- **Online Portal:** https://apps.wv.gov/sos/ucc/
- UCC Online Filing, Search & Retrieval System available 24/7
- Users can submit filings, search database, order certified/non-certified searches, and download documents
- Payment: Credit card only for online transactions
- Mail: Not accepted for filings (online only since 2016)
- In-person: Not applicable (online system required)

**Contact Information:**
- **Phone:** 304-558-8000 or Toll-free: 866-767-8683
- **Email:** UCC@wvsos.gov
- **Website:** https://sos.wv.gov/business/Pages/UCCFilingProc.aspx

**Confidence Level:** High - All information verified from official state sources

### 2. WISCONSIN

**Filing Office:** Wisconsin Department of Financial Institutions (DFI), Division of Corporate and Consumer Services

**Authorization Model:** **Open Access with Free Account Registration** (No formal authorized searcher program)
- Confidence Level: High
- No authorized searcher certification program exists
- Free user account required for full system access
- Account can be created same-day
- Both individual and organization registrations available

**Key Fees:**
- Electronic UCC Filing: $10.00
- Realty-related UCC recordings: $30.00 per document (regardless of pages)
- Free UCC Search: Available without login from homepage
- UCC-11 Information Request: Paid search (requires login) - specific fee not disclosed in public sources
- Bulk data extracts: Available weekly

**Access Method:**
- **Online Portal:** https://dccs.wdfi.org/ (DFI Online Integrated Processing System)
- **Alternative Portal:** https://wims.dfi.wi.gov/
- Free searches available without login
- Full filing and certified search capabilities require free account
- Mail: Available for certain transactions
- In-person: Available at physical location

**Contact Information:**

### 3. WYOMING

**Filing Office:** Wyoming Secretary of State, Business Division

- Confidence Level: Medium-High

- Requires registration and prepaid account for e-filing system
- Buyer/Subscriber registration system rather than searcher certification
- Valid for one year, renewable

**Key Fees:**

  - UCC1 (more than 2 pages): $60.00

### 4. DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA

**Filing Office:** DC Office of Tax and Revenue, Recorder of Deeds (ROD)

**Authorization Model:** **Open Access with Registration** (No formal authorized searcher program)
- Confidence Level: Medium-High
- No formal authorized searcher certification program found
- Public search available to registered users and subscribers
- DC Recorder of Deeds does not provide certified searches
- UCC filings split between:
  - Fixture filings (real property): Filed in land records
  - Chattel filings: Filed in chattel records

**Key Fees:**

## Summary Analysis

**Authorization Programs:**
- **None of the four jurisdictions** operate formal "authorized searcher" certification programs similar to Delaware's system
- All provide varying degrees of open/public access to UCC searches
- Wyoming has the most restrictive model with subscription and prepaid account requirements
- West Virginia, Wisconsin, and DC offer more open access models with free or low-barrier registration

**Access Models:**
1. **West Virginia:** Most streamlined - fully online, mandatory electronic filing
2. **Wisconsin:** Hybrid - free searches available, account required for full access
3. **Wyoming:** Subscription-based - requires annual fee and prepaid account
4. **DC:** Open registration - free search/view, pay per download

**Fee Ranges:**
- Electronic UCC1 filings: $10.00 (WI) to $20.00 (WV)
- Paper filings (where accepted): $30.00-$60.00 (WY)
- Search fees: Free to $10.00 for basic searches
- Download/copy fees: $2.25-$4.00 per document (DC)
