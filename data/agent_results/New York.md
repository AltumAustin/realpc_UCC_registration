- Credit card payment authorization required

### 3. REQUIREMENTS

**Confidence Level:** ✓✓✓ CONFIRMED

**For Search Access:**
- No special requirements for public online searches
- For certified searches: Submit Form UCC-11 with payment

**For Filing Access:**

*Basic E-Filing (Web Portal):*
- Valid email address (mandatory field)
- Authorized credit card (MasterCard, Visa, or American Express only)
- Debtor authorization for the filing

*XML/API Bulk Filing:*
- Completed "Application to Establish a Drawdown Account"
- Minimum startup deposit of $100
- Successful test XML file submission
- Client ID and Password (issued by NYS DOS after approval)
- Prepaid drawdown account for processing fees

*Required Filing Information:*
- For business debtors: Exact legal name as registered with NYS DOS, organization type, organizational ID number
- For individual debtors: Legal name as appears on unexpired driver's license or ID
- Full legal name and mailing address of secured party/creditor
- Clear, specific description of collateral
- Note: National Standard UCC forms NOT acceptable when debtor is an organization (NY-specific forms required)

### 4. ACCESS METHOD

**Confidence Level:** ✓✓✓ CONFIRMED

**Search Methods:**

*Free Public Online Search:*
- **URL:** https://appext20.dos.ny.gov/pls/ucc_public/web_search.menu_frame
- **Features:** 4 search options available:

  - Other Debtor Search Options
- **Database:** Complete UCC database including Financing Statements, Amendments (Article 9 and Revised Article 9), and Federal Tax Liens (Article 10-A)
- **Availability:** 7 days/week
- **Output:** Printable data reports and/or viewable images of filed records

*Certified Search:*
- Submit Form UCC-11 with $25 fee via mail or online

**Filing Methods:**

*Web-Based E-Filing Portal:*
- **URL:** https://appext20.dos.ny.gov/pls/efiling_public/ucc_app.eucc.euccFiling
- **Process:** Access form template → Enter information → Click "File" → Submit with payment
- **Payment:** Credit card only (MC/Visa/Amex)
- **Confirmation:** Email acknowledgment receipt and copy of filed image

*XML Bulk Filing:*
- **URL:** https://appext20.dos.ny.gov/ASPUCCXML/
- **Method:** REST API with XML data structure
- **Authentication:** Client ID and Password (issued credentials)
- **Payment:** Prepaid drawdown account
- **Confirmation:** Electronic acknowledgment with filing number for each state filing

*Mail Submission:*
- Paper-based forms mailed to Albany address above

## SUMMARY

New York operates an **open-access UCC system** with minimal barriers:

- **Searches:** Completely open to the public via free online portal, no registration required
- **Filings:** Open to anyone with valid payment method (credit card or prepaid account)
- **High-Volume Access:** Available through XML API with drawdown account and issued credentials
