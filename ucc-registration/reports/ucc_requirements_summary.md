# UCC Requirements Summary — First 10 States (AL through GA)

**Prepared for:** RealPC Real Finance, Inc.
**Date:** February 14, 2026
**Prepared by:** Automated research system

---

## Executive Summary

Research has been completed for the first 10 states alphabetically (Alabama through Georgia). Of these 10 jurisdictions:

- **7 states** have fully open public access (no authorization needed to search)
- **2 states** require subscription/account registration (straightforward online signup)
- **1 state** (Delaware) has a mandatory Authorized Searcher program that may be closed to new applicants — **this is the only significant blocker identified**

**Estimated annual cost for all 10 states:** ~$450/year (Arkansas $150 + Georgia $180 + Alabama $120 optional)
