### 2. AUTHORIZATION MODEL
**Model Type:** **Open Access with Account Registration**  
**Confidence Level:** ✓ **HIGH** (Confirmed via administrative code and official sources)

**Key Characteristics:**
- **For Searches:** Completely open access - no account required for basic online searches
- **For Filings:** Simple account registration required (not a formal authorization program)
- **Philosophy:** Iowa operates as a filing repository and does not pre-screen or determine filer authority

**Account Types:**
- **Standard Online Account:** Available to anyone with email address
- **XML Transmission Authorization:** Requires written authorization from filing officer and formal agreement (for bulk/automated filers)

### SUMMARY OF KEY FINDINGS

**Iowa's UCC system is characterized by:**

1. **Open Access Model** - No formal authorization program or restrictive eligibility requirements
2. **Low Barrier to Entry** - Simple email-based account registration for online filing
3. **No Professional Requirements** - No bonding, insurance, background checks, or training mandated
4. **Cost-Effective** - $5 online filing fee (among the lowest nationally); free basic searches
5. **Modern Online System** - Full-featured Fast Track Filing portal for 24/7 access
6. **Non-Judgmental Filing** - State acts as repository only; does not verify filer authority
7. **National Form Compliance** - Requires IACA-approved UCC forms

**Operational Philosophy:**  
Iowa takes a "file and let the courts sort it out" approach, placing responsibility on filers to ensure proper authorization rather than implementing a gatekeeping system.
