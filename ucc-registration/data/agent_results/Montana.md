## MONTANA UCC SEARCHER/FILING ACCESS REQUIREMENTS

### 1. FILING OFFICE
**Montana Secretary of State - Business Services Division**
- **Confidence Level: HIGH** ✓
- The Montana Secretary of State is the central filing office for all UCC liens in the state
- Secretary: Christi Jacobsen

### 2. AUTHORIZATION MODEL
**Secured Party Authorization Model**
- **Confidence Level: HIGH** ✓
- For UCC liens: The secured party of record must authorize the filing
- For EFS (Effective Financing Statement) liens: The debtor must sign, authorize, or authenticate the filing, and the document must be filed by the secured party under the Federal Act
- Authorization requirements are governed by Montana Administrative Rules 44.6.113

### 3. REQUIREMENTS

**Filing Requirements:**
- **Confidence Level: HIGH** ✓
- All UCC liens MUST be filed electronically via the Secretary of State's Business Services Division website
- Paper filings are not accepted for UCC liens
- Online filing is available 24/7 except during scheduled maintenance
- User account creation required at biz.sosmt.gov for filing services
- Payment by credit or debit card only

**Search Access Requirements:**
- **Confidence Level: HIGH** ✓
- NOT open/free access - fees required for searches
- Two options available:
  - Pay-per-search: $7.00 per debtor or secured party name
  - Subscription service: $25.00 per month for unlimited searches

### 4. ACCESS METHOD

**Filing Portal:**
- **URL:** https://biz.sosmt.gov
- **Confidence Level: HIGH** ✓
- Electronic filing system available online
- Requires user profile creation and login
- Effective date/time recorded when successfully filed in UCC database

**Search Portal:**
- **URL:** https://biz.sosmt.gov/search/ucc
- **Confidence Level: HIGH** ✓
- Online UCC Search allows users to:
  - Search debtor names
  - View filing chains
  - Download debtor search certificates
  - Download images of lien documents (additional fee applies)

### 5. FEE SCHEDULE
**Confidence Level: HIGH** ✓

**Filing Fees (per Montana Administrative Rule 44.6.105, effective 9/7/2019):**
- Initial Financing Statement: $7.00
- Continuation Statement: $5.00
- Assignment: $5.00
- Statement of Partial Release: $5.00
- Statement Adding/Changing/Deleting Collateral: $5.00
- Statement Adding/Changing/Deleting Debtor Information: $5.00
- Statement Adding/Changing/Deleting Secured Party Information: $5.00
- Any Other Amendment: $5.00

**Search Fees:**
- Search Certificate (per name): $7.00
- List and Copies (to see collateral/copies): Additional $5.00
- Certification of Copies: $2.00 (included if copies accompany search certificate)

**Subscription Services:**
- UCC Standard Subscription: $25.00/month (unlimited debtor search certificates)
- Farm Bill Subscription: $20.00/month (unlimited searches of farm bill master list)
- UCC Bulk Subscription: $1,000.00/month (daily downloads of all lien filings and images)
- Public Access Hookup Fee: $25.00/month (per ARM 44.6.105)
