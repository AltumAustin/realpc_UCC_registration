## Missouri UCC Searcher/Filing Access Requirements - Research Findings

### 1. FILING OFFICE
**Primary Office:** Missouri Secretary of State, Uniform Commercial Code Division  
**Confidence Level:** ✓✓✓ HIGH (Confirmed via multiple official sources)

**Details:**
- As of July 1, 2001, the Missouri Secretary of State assumed duties for all UCC filings (except real estate-related fixture filings)
- Real estate-related UCC filings must be filed at the county recorder's office where the debtor's real property is located
- All other UCC filings are centralized with the Secretary of State

### 5. FEE SCHEDULE
**Confidence Level:** ✓✓ MEDIUM (Based on 2024-2025 sources; official 2026 page inaccessible)

**Filing Fees (Current as of 2024-2025):**
- UCC-1 Initial Financing Statement: $15 (electronic) / $25 (paper)
- UCC-3 Continuation: $15 (electronic) / $25 (paper)
- UCC-3 Amendment: $15 (electronic) / $25 (paper)
- UCC-3 Termination: $15 (electronic) / $25 (paper)
- Additional fee: $2 per page for paper filings exceeding 2 pages

**Note:** Missouri increased electronic filing fees from $10 to $15 in early 2024.

**Search Fees:**
- Online search/viewing: FREE
- Certified/paper copies: Minimum $17 fee

**Processing Times:**
- Electronic filings: Same day or next business day
- Mail filings: 3-5 business days
- No expedited fee option (expedited processing not offered)
