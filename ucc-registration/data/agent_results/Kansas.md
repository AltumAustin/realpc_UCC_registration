### 2. AUTHORIZATION MODEL
**Model Type:** **Subscription-Based Online Portal with Public Access Alternative**

**Details:**
- **Online Access:** Requires free subscription/entity account to access the online UCC portal
- **Paper Filing:** Available to anyone without account (mail-in option)
- **No Formal Authorization Program:** Kansas does not operate a formal credentialing or authorization program for UCC filing agents
- **Open Eligibility:** Both businesses and individuals can create accounts

**Confidence Level:** ✓ Confirmed

### 3. REQUIREMENTS

#### A. **Forms Required**
- UCC entity account application form (for online access)
- UCC1 Financing Statement (for filings)
- UCC3 Financing Statement Amendment (for amendments)

#### B. **Fees**
- **Account Application:** FREE
- No setup fees, activation fees, or annual subscription costs

#### C. **Eligibility**
- **Open to:** Both businesses and individuals
- **No Restrictions:** No special licensing, certification, or business registration required
- Account name can be business name or individual name
- Account name is not legally linked to filed/searched UCCs

#### D. **Documents Required for Account Application**
- Entity Name (Company Name) or Individual Name
- Contact Name
- Mailing Address (Address 1, City, State)
- Email Address
- **No Background Checks Required**
- **No Business Documentation Required**
- **No Bonding Required**
- **No Insurance Required**

#### E. **Background Checks**
**Not Required** for UCC filing/search access

*Note: Background checks and bonding mentioned in search results relate to insurance agents (particularly title insurance agents), not UCC filing agents*

#### F. **Technical Requirements**
- Internet access for online portal
- Email address for account activation
- Ability to accept electronic communications
- Payment method: ACH, electronic check, or credit card (for online filings)

#### G. **Training**
**Not Required** - No mandatory training programs

#### H. **Bonding/Insurance**
**Not Required** for UCC filing or search access

### 5. FEE SCHEDULE

#### A. **Search Fees**

| Search Type | Fee |
|------------|-----|
| **Online Unofficial Debtor Search** | $10 per debtor name |
| **Online Official Debtor Search** | $10 per debtor name |
| **Paper Official Debtor Search Request** | $20 per debtor name |
| **Collateral Image Copies** | $1 per page |
| **Additional Debtors** | No charge |

**Note:** Unofficial searches are nonstandard and do not follow standard search logic as listed in K.A.R. 7-17-22

#### B. **Filing Fees**

| Filing Type | Online Fee | Paper Fee |
|------------|-----------|-----------|
| **UCC1 Financing Statement** | $10 + $1 per page after first 10 pages | $20 + $1 per page after first 10 pages |
| **UCC1 (Transmitting Utilities)** | $50 + $1 per page after first 10 pages | $50 + $1 per page after first 10 pages |
| **UCC3 Financing Statement Amendment** | $10 + $1 per page after first 10 pages | $20 + $1 per page after first 10 pages |

**Payment Methods:**
- **Online:** ACH, electronic check, or credit card
- **Paper:** Check, money order, or credit card

**Additional Information:**
- Financing statements are effective for **5 years** from date of filing
- Will lapse after 5 years unless continuation statement is filed prior to lapse date

## KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY

1. **Low Barrier to Entry:** Kansas has one of the most accessible UCC systems - free account creation with minimal information required
2. **No Professional Requirements:** No licensing, bonding, insurance, background checks, or training required
3. **Dual Access Model:** Both online (preferred) and paper filing options available
4. **Cost Incentive for Online Filing:** Online filings are 50% cheaper than paper filings ($10 vs $20 base fee)
5. **Quick Activation:** 2-3 business days to receive account credentials
6. **Open to All:** Both individuals and businesses can create accounts and file/search UCCs
