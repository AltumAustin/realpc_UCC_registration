## Kentucky UCC Searcher/Filing Access Requirements - Research Findings

### 1. FILING OFFICE
**Agency:** Kentucky Secretary of State, UCC Division  
**Confidence Level:** HIGH

The Kentucky Secretary of State serves as the state repository for UCC financing statements regarding security interests under Kentucky Revised Statutes Chapter 355, Revised Article 9.

**Exception:** Fixture filings, as-extracted mineral, or timber to be cut filings must be filed with County Clerks. Motor vehicle title liens are also handled at the county level.

---

### 2. AUTHORIZATION MODEL
**Model:** Open Access Public Portal  
**Confidence Level:** HIGH

Kentucky operates an **open access system** with no formal authorization program or special registration required:
- **UCC Searches:** Free public access, no account required, available 24/7
- **UCC Filings:** Open to anyone, though typically used by secured creditors (banks, mortgage companies, lending institutions)
- **Optional Prepaid Account:** Available for frequent filers but not required

---

### 3. REQUIREMENTS

#### A. For Basic Search Access
**Confidence Level:** HIGH
- **None** - Public access is unrestricted and free

#### B. For Filing Access
**Confidence Level:** HIGH
- **No special authorization required** - Anyone can file
- **Payment method required** - Credit/debit card, ACH, prepaid account, check, or cash

#### C. For Prepaid Account (Optional)
**Confidence Level:** HIGH
- Application for Prepaid Account form
- Minimum deposit: $250
- List of authorized users (if business entity)
- Account number for future deposits
- Responsibility to maintain sufficient balance

#### D. Other Requirements
**Confidence Level:** HIGH
- **Forms:** Must use proper UCC forms (UCC-1 for initial filings, UCC-3 for amendments)
- **Debtor Information:** Exact legal name required (must match Secretary of State business records for registered entities)
- **Secured Party Information:** Complete legal name and address
- **Collateral Description:** Clear description of collateral
- **No Background Checks:** None required
- **No Bonding:** None required
- **No Special Training:** None required
- **Technical Requirements:** Internet access and web browser for online portal; credit/debit card for online payments

### 5. FEE SCHEDULE
**Confidence Level:** HIGH

#### Search Fees
- **Online Index Search:** FREE
- **Certified Search:** $5.00 per certificate per file number + $0.50 per page for copies

#### Filing Fees
- **Online UCC-1 (Initial Financing Statement):** $5.00
- **Online UCC-3 (Amendment):** $5.00
- **Paper Filing (Mail):** $20.00 + $2.00 processing fee = $22.00 total
- **Standard Paper Filing:** $20.00

#### Payment Methods
- Credit/debit card (online)
- Electronic Funds Transfer (ACH) for online filings
- Prepaid account
- Check made payable to Kentucky State Treasurer
- Cash (in-person only)

#### Additional Services
- **Certified Copies:** $5.00 per certificate per file number
- **Copy Pages:** $0.50 per page accompanying certificate
- **Continuation Statement:** Filed within six-month period before expiration (5-year effectiveness period)
