### 1. PENNSYLVANIA

**1) Filing Office:**
- Pennsylvania Department of State, Bureau of Corporations and Charitable Organizations
- Address: Uniform Commercial Code, PO Box 8721, Harrisburg, PA 17105-8721

**2) Authorization Model:**
- **Open Access** - No formal authorized searcher program identified
- Public access available through online portal and traditional methods
- Anyone can search and file through the online system

**3) Key Fees:**
- **Filing Fees:**
  - UCC-1 Financing Statement: $84.00 (paper or online)
  - UCC-3 Amendment/Continuation: $84.00
  - No additional fee for attachments or additional debtors
- **Search Fees:**
  - Information Request (UCC-11): $12.00 per debtor name search
  - Each additional debtor name: $6.00
- **Expedited Services:**
  - Same-day service: $100
  - 3-hour service: $300
  - 1-hour service: $500

**4) Access Methods:**
- **Online Portal:** https://file.dos.pa.gov/search/ucc (free public search)
- **Online Filing:** https://hub.business.pa.gov/ (PA Business One-Stop Hub - requires login)
- **Mail:** Available for paper submissions
- **In-Person:** Department of State office in Harrisburg

**5) Contact Information:**
- **Phone:** 717-787-1057 (option 3 for UCC)
- **Email:** Not specified in search results
- **Website:** https://www.pa.gov/agencies/dos/resources/business-resources/uniform-commercial-code

## SUMMARY ANALYSIS

### Authorization Models by State:

1. **Open Access (3 states):** Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, Tennessee
   - No formal authorization or certification program required
   - Public access available to all users
   
2. **Subscription Programs (2 states):** South Carolina, South Dakota
   - **South Carolina:** $75 annual subscription (optional, provides monthly billing convenience)
   - **South Dakota:** Subscription pricing not publicly disclosed ("Dakota Fast File Program")

### Fee Ranges:

- **UCC-1 Filing Fees:** $8-$84 (Rhode Island electronic $8, Pennsylvania $84)
- **Search Fees:** $5-$20 (Rhode Island $5, Tennessee $15, South Dakota ~$20)
- **Certified Searches:** Tennessee automatically certifies all searches at no additional cost

### Online Access Quality:

- **Most Advanced:** South Carolina (24/7 subscriber system, API access)
- **Most User-Friendly:** Tennessee (automatic certification, online wizard, immediate PDF retrieval)
- **Most Affordable:** Rhode Island (free non-certified searches, $5 certified searches, $8 e-filing)

### Data Completeness:

- **Complete Fee Information:** Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, Tennessee
- **Partial Fee Information:** South Carolina (subscription price known, transaction fees not disclosed)
- **Limited Fee Information:** South Dakota (must contact office for current rates)
