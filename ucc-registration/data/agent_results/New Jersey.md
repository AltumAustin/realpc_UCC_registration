## NEW JERSEY UCC SEARCHER/FILING ACCESS REQUIREMENTS - RESEARCH FINDINGS

### 1) FILING OFFICE
**Office:** New Jersey Department of the Treasury, Division of Revenue and Enterprise Services (DORES)  
**Filing Officer:** Treasurer of the State of New Jersey  
**Confidence Level:** HIGH ✓

The Division of Revenue & Enterprise Services is the official state filing office for all UCC financing statements in New Jersey.

---

### 2) AUTHORIZATION MODEL
**Model:** Open Access with Optional Account Registration  
**Confidence Level:** HIGH ✓

New Jersey operates on an **open access model** with the following characteristics:

- **No pre-authorization required** for basic access to search or file
- **Optional Payment Management Services (PMS) account** available for frequent users
- PMS accounts are **free to create** - users can sign up by clicking the "New User" link
- Account benefits include:
  - Securely save payment information
  - Manage multiple users
  - View activity reports
  - Streamlined filing process

---

### 3) REQUIREMENTS

**Filing Requirements:**
- **Electronic filing is mandatory** for all UCC filings, search requests, and fee payments unless exempted
- **Exemption process:** Written request must be mailed to:
  - NJ Division of Revenue
  - 33 West State Street, 5th Floor
  - Trenton, NJ 08608
  - Attn: UCC Filing Officer

**Account Requirements (for PMS users):**
- Free registration through njportal.com
- Payment information (for processing fees)
- No licensing or credentials required

**Confidence Level:** HIGH ✓

---

### 4) ACCESS METHOD

**Primary Access:** Online Portal  
**URL:** https://www.njportal.com/ucc/  
**Alternative URL:** https://www.nj.gov/treasury/revenue/

**Services Available:**
- Submit UCC-1 (initial financing statements)
- Submit UCC-3 (amendments, continuations, terminations)
- Conduct certified searches (based on Revised Article 9 rules)
- Conduct non-certified informational searches (with wildcards and partial string searches)
- Download documents directly to computer
- Electronic confirmation for all filings
- Downloadable filed forms in national standard format

**Search Types:**
- **Certified Search:** Based on RA9 rules, requires specification of debtor type (organizational vs. individual)
- **Non-Certified Search:** Informational search allowing wildcards and partial string searches

**Alternative Methods:**
- Mail-in services (if exempted from electronic filing)
- Over-the-counter services (if exempted)

**Confidence Level:** HIGH ✓

---

### 5) FEE SCHEDULE

**Filing Fees:**
- UCC-1 (Initial Financing Statement): **$25.00** (statutory fee) + **$5.00** (portal admin fee) = **$30.00 total**
- UCC-3 (Amendment): **$25.00** (statutory fee) + **$5.00** (portal admin fee) = **$30.00 total**

**Search Fees:**
- Certified Search Certificate with photocopies: **$25.00** per certificate + **$1.00** per page + **$5.00** (portal admin fee)

**Payment Methods:**
- Online: Credit/debit card through PMS account or per-transaction payment
- Mail/In-Person: Personal check, cashier's check, or certified check payable to "Treasurer, State of New Jersey"

**Confidence Level:** HIGH ✓

### SUMMARY

New Jersey operates a **modern, electronic-first UCC system** with open public access. The system does not require pre-authorization or credentials for basic use, though frequent users may benefit from creating a free PMS account. All services are primarily delivered through the state's online portal at njportal.com/ucc, with mandatory electronic filing unless specifically exempted. The fee structure is straightforward with a $25 statutory fee plus $5 portal administration fee for most transactions.
