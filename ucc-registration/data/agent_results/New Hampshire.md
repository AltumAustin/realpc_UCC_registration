## New Hampshire UCC Searcher/Filing Access Requirements - Research Findings

### 1. FILING OFFICE
**Primary Office:** New Hampshire Secretary of State - UCC Division

**Confidence Level:** HIGH ✓

**Details:**
- **Mailing Address:** Uniform Commercial Code, N.H. Department of State, 107 North Main Street, Concord, NH 03301-4989
- **Physical Location:** State House Annex Room 313, 25 Capital Street, 3rd Floor, Concord, NH 03301
- **Jurisdiction:** All UCC filings dated July 1, 2001 and later are filed with the Secretary of State. Pre-July 2001 filings are maintained at local city/town clerk offices.

---

### 2. AUTHORIZATION MODEL
**Model Type:** Account-Based Registration System (NH Quickstart)

**Confidence Level:** HIGH ✓

**Details:**
- **Online Filing:** Requires a free NH Quickstart account
- **Account Types Available:**
  - **UCC User:** For high-volume filers who predominantly file UCCs
  - **Business User:** For owners/officers filing on behalf of a single business
  - **Registered Agent User:** For those filing on behalf of multiple businesses
  - **Other User:** For occasional filers
- **Paper Filing:** No account required for paper submissions
- **Public Access:** Search services available to anyone (with fees)

---

### 3. REQUIREMENTS

**Confidence Level:** HIGH ✓

**For Online Filing:**
- Free NH Quickstart account registration at https://quickstart.sos.nh.gov/online/Registration
- Acknowledgment address must be provided in section B of filing forms
- Compliance with N.H. RSA 382-A:9-526 and UCC Section 9-516

**For Paper Filing:**
- Completed forms (available in PDF format on Secretary of State website)
- Acknowledgment address in section B ("Send Acknowledgement To:")
- Appropriate fees enclosed
- Return envelopes no longer required (as of current rules)

**Processing Standards:**
- State law requires UCC filings be processed within 3 business days of receipt
- Expedited service available for additional fee

---

### 4. ACCESS METHOD

**Confidence Level:** HIGH ✓

**Online Access:**
- **Filing Portal:** NH Quickstart system at https://quickstart.sos.nh.gov
- **Search Access:** Through Secretary of State's office (fee-based, not free public access)
- **Account Creation:** https://quickstart.sos.nh.gov/online/Registration

**Paper/Mail Access:**
- Mail submissions to: Uniform Commercial Code, N.H. Department of State, 107 North Main Street, Concord, NH 03301-4989
- Search requests can be submitted by mail

- State House Annex Room 313, 25 Capital Street, 3rd Floor, Concord, NH 03301

### 5. FEE SCHEDULE

**Confidence Level:** HIGH ✓

**Filing Fees:**
- **Standard Filing (1-2 pages):** $48.00
- **Additional Pages:** $83.00 total
- **Expedited Service:** Additional $50.00 (for exact-time filing)
- **Electronic Transaction Fee:** Additional $2.00 handling charge for all fees received electronically (effective July 1, 2004)

**Search Fees:**
- **Routine UCC Search:** $10.00
- **Certified Search (24-hour results):** $25.00
- **Local Searches (pre-July 1, 2001 records):** $10.00 per search at city clerk offices, plus $1.00 per page copied (varies by municipality)

### Additional Notes:

1. **No Pre-Authorization Required:** New Hampshire does not appear to require special credentials or pre-approval to conduct UCC searches or file documents beyond creating a free Quickstart account for online access.

2. **Recent Rules:** The filing office rules are based on the UCC Model Rules (Rev. 9 May 2018) from the International Association of Commercial Administrators.

3. **Historical Records:** Records filed before July 1, 2001 must be searched at local city/town clerk offices, not the Secretary of State.

4. **No Free Online Search:** Unlike some states, New Hampshire does not offer free public online UCC search access - all searches require payment.
