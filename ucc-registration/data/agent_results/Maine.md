## 2. AUTHORIZATION MODEL
**Model Type:** **Public Portal with Optional Subscription Enhancement**

Maine operates an **open access system** with tiered service options:
- **Public Access (Pay-per-use):** Anyone can file and search UCC documents using a credit card
- **InforME Subscription:** Enhanced features available for subscribers with additional benefits
- **Government Agency Fee Waiver:** Government entities can qualify for free subscription accounts (subject to Secretary of State approval), though these accounts cannot access UCC Filing service

## 5. FEE SCHEDULE

### 5.1 Filing Fees (Paper Submissions)

**UCC-1 Financing Statement:**
- 1-2 pages: **$15.00**
- More than 2 pages: **$30.00**
- Public Finance Transaction: **$60.00**
- Manufactured-Home Transaction: **$40.00**

**UCC-3 Amendment (continuation, assignment, amendment, termination):**
- 1-2 pages: **$15.00**
- More than 2 pages: **$30.00**
- Termination only (for UCC-1 filed July 1, 1993 - July 1, 2001): **No fee**

**Confidence Level:** ✓✓✓ High
