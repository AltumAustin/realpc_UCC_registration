- **Background Checks:** None required
- **Bonding:** None required

## **SUMMARY**

Indiana operates a **modern, hybrid UCC access system** through its INBiz portal. The state provides **free, open public access** for UCC searches without requiring any account or authorization. However, **filing UCC documents requires creating a free Access Indiana account** linked to the INBiz system. 

The authorization model is **subscription-based but publicly accessible** - anyone can create an account without special eligibility requirements, background checks, or bonding. The system is primarily **online-focused** with electronic filing as the preferred method, though traditional paper filing by mail or in-person remains available.

Fees are **relatively modest** ($23 for filings, free for searches, $20 for certified documents), and the technical requirements are minimal (just a modern web browser and email address). Indiana's approach represents a **modern, accessible, low-barrier system** for UCC transactions.
