### 2. AUTHORIZATION MODEL
**Model:** **Open Access / Self-Registration**

Minnesota operates an open-access system with no special authorization, certification, or licensing required to conduct UCC searches or file UCC statements. Any individual or entity can create a free online account and immediately access the system.

**Key Characteristics:**
- No pre-approval or vetting process required
- No certified searcher program identified
- No professional service provider registration required
- "Certified Search" refers to the search product type (UCC Standard Search), not to individual searchers
- Simple self-service account creation with email/password

**Exception:** Bulk data purchases require a signed license agreement (see details below).

**Confidence Level:** **HIGH** - Based on comprehensive search of official sources and absence of any authorization requirements in published materials

### 3. REQUIREMENTS

#### A. **Forms Required**
**Standard Access:**
- No forms required for basic online account creation
- UCC11 form (Information Request - IACA form) required for UCC Standard or Limited Search requests

**Bulk Data Access:**
- MBLS Business Services Bulk Data Request for Commercial Users

### 4. ACCESS METHOD

**Primary Method:** Online through Minnesota Business & Lien System (MBLS) portal

**Portal URL:** https://mblsportal.sos.mn.gov/

**Account Creation Process:**
1. Visit the account creation page and enter email address
2. Create a password meeting requirements (minimum 8 characters, at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number)
3. Accept Terms and Conditions
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Wait for confirmation email from NoReply-MNSecretaryOfState-BusinessServices@state.mn.us (link valid 24 hours)
6. Click confirmation link to verify email address
7. Log in with credentials

**Alternative Methods:**
- Paper submissions accepted (same fees as online)
- Phone inquiries: (651) 296-2803 or 1-877-551-6767

**Search Options Available:**
- **UCC Standard Search** - "Certified Search" admissible in court, includes all exact matches on debtor name
- **UCC Limited Search** - Can filter by city or date range, NOT admissible in court
- **Secured Party Search** - Search by secured party name
- **Search by Filing Number** - Direct lookup of specific filing

### KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY

1. **No Authorization Barriers:** Minnesota operates an open-access system with minimal barriers to entry
2. **Free Account Creation:** Anyone can create an account at no cost
3. **Flat Fee Structure:** Simple $20 fee for most transactions
4. **No Special Requirements:** No background checks, bonding, training, or certification needed
5. **Online-First:** Fully functional online portal with same fees as paper submissions
6. **Immediate Access:** Account creation and confirmation typically completed within 24 hours
7. **No Certified Searcher Program:** Unlike some states, Minnesota does not maintain a list of certified or authorized searchers

### COMPARISON NOTE
Minnesota's approach is notably more open and accessible than states with formal authorization programs. There are no gatekeeping mechanisms, special credentials, or ongoing compliance requirements for basic search and filing access.
