## Hawaii UCC Filing and Search Access Requirements - Research Findings

### 1. FILING OFFICE
**Confidence Level: HIGH**

**Agency:** Bureau of Conveyances (BOC), Department of Land and Natural Resources (DLNR)
- Hawaii is the **only state in the nation with a single statewide recording office**
- The Bureau handles all UCC filings (Hawaii does not have a Secretary of State)
- **Unique Feature:** All UCC documents are recorded centrally, unlike other states with county-level systems

**Key Officials:**
- Registrar: Mike H. Imanaka
- Deputy Registrar: Bess Palma

---

### 2. AUTHORIZATION MODEL
**Confidence Level: HIGH**

**Hybrid Model - Three Access Tiers:**

**A. Public Portal (Open Access)**
- Anyone can create a free user account to search UCC records
- Searching is free; fees apply only when ordering/downloading documents
- Available 24/7 at https://bocdataext.hi.wcicloud.com/

**B. Pay-Per-Use Access**
- Credit card payment option for individual transactions
- No subscription required
- Suitable for occasional users

**C. Monthly Subscriber Account (Formal Program)**
- Enhanced access with monthly billing
- Requires formal application and approval
- Two subscription tiers available

---

### 3. REQUIREMENTS
**Confidence Level: HIGH**

**For Basic Public Access:**
- Create a user account (free registration)
- Credit card for pay-per-use transactions
- No special eligibility requirements
- No background checks or bonding required

**For Monthly Subscriber Account:**

**Initial Setup Fee:** $500
- **Annual Renewal:** $500 (billed automatically)

**Documentation Required:**
- **Organizations:** Valid Hawaii Certificate of Good Standing (COGS) OR equivalent from another state
- **Sole Proprietors:** Copy of General Excise Tax license
- Completed and signed Subscriber Agreement

**User Management:**
- Can assign up to 10 user names per account
- Account holder responsible for all charges and actions

**Application Process:**
1. Read and complete Subscriber Agreement
2. Submit signed Agreement with Certificate of Good Standing
3. Submit payment ($500) via check or money order to "Bureau of Conveyances"
4. Bureau contacts applicant with usernames and passwords
5. Begin using services

**No Requirements For:**
- Background checks
- Bonding
- Special training or certification
- Technical certifications
- Security clearances

### 5. FEE SCHEDULE
**Confidence Level: HIGH**

**UCC Filing Fees (Effective since February 27, 2017):**
- **UCC1 (Financing Statement):** $41.00 per document
- **UCC3 (Amendment):** $41.00 per document
- **UCC5 (Correction Statement):** $41.00 per document
- **Addendum:** $41.00 per document (included in base fee)
- **Additional Party:** $41.00 per document (included in base fee)

**UCC Search Fees:**
- **UCC11 (Information Request):** $25.00 base fee per search
- **Additional creditor found:** $5.00 each
- **Copy charges:** $1.00 per page
- **Certification:** $10.00 (if required)

**Document Access Fees (Search Portal):**

**Pay-Per-Document:**
- Credit card users: $1.00 per page (plus service fees)
- Example: 5-page document = $5.00 + service fees

**Monthly Subscription Plans:**
- **Unlimited Access:** $1,000/month (unlimited document downloads)
- **Public Tier:** $50/month + $3.00 per document downloaded

**Subscriber Account Fees:**
- **Initial Setup:** $500
- **Annual Renewal:** $500
