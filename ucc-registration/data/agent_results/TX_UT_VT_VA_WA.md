## UCC SEARCHER/FILING ACCESS REQUIREMENTS - FIVE STATE RESEARCH REPORT

### 1. TEXAS

**Filing Office:** Texas Secretary of State, Uniform Commercial Code Section

**Authorization Model:** Open access with required account registration
- **Type:** Mandatory online portal system (as of August 29, 2025 - no paper filings accepted)
- **Access Level:** Public access - anyone can create an SOS Portal account
- **No formal "authorized searcher" program identified**

**Key Fees:**
- UCC Search: $1.00 per search (statutory fee)
- UCC-1 Filing (online): $15 for 2 pages or less; $30 for 3+ pages
- Special transactions: $60 (public-finance, manufactured-home, transmitting utility)
- Expedited processing: Additional $15 per filing or request

**Access Method:**
- **Online Portal:** SOS Portal (https://www.sos.state.tx.us/) - REQUIRED for all filings as of 2026
- **In-Person:** 400 W. 15th Street, Austin, TX 78701 (lobby hours: 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM, Mon-Fri; walk-in service Mon & Fri)
- **Mail:** P.O. Box 13193, Austin, TX 78711-3193 (search/info requests only - no filings)

**Contact Information:**
- **Phone:** Office hours Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- **Email:** sosdirect@sos.texas.gov, response@sos.texas.gov
- **Website:** https://www.sos.state.tx.us/ucc/index.shtml

**Confidence Level:** HIGH - Information verified through official Texas SOS sources

### 5. WASHINGTON

**Filing Office:** Washington State Department of Licensing (NOT Secretary of State)

**Authorization Model:** Open access - no account registration required for searches
- **Type:** Public access online system
- **Access Level:** Open to public; searches available without account

## SUMMARY FINDINGS

**Authorization Models Across All Five States:**
- **ALL STATES:** Open access model - no formal "authorized searcher" programs identified
- **ALL STATES:** Require some form of account registration for FILING, but not necessarily for basic searching
- **Texas:** Most restrictive - mandatory online portal for ALL filings (no paper accepted since Aug 2025)
- **Utah & Washington:** Free public searches available online
- **Vermont:** Free uncertified searches; paid certified searches
- **Virginia:** CIS account required with identity verification

**Common Patterns:**
1. All states have moved to online filing systems with portal registration
2. None of the five states maintain formal "authorized searcher" certification programs
3. Search fees range from FREE (Utah, Washington basic searches) to $20 per debtor (Vermont certified)
4. Filing fees range from $10 (Virginia, Washington) to $12-30 (Utah, Texas)
5. All states accept credit card payments online; fees are generally non-refundable

**Access Confidence:**
- Texas, Utah, Virginia, Washington: HIGH confidence (verified through official state sources)
- Vermont: MEDIUM-HIGH confidence (most information verified; some fee details not fully documented)
