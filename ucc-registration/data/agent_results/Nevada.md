### 2. AUTHORIZATION MODEL
**Model:** Account-Based Registration with Optional Trust Account  
**Confidence Level:** HIGH (95%)

**Requirements:**
- **Primary Access:** Users must create a SilverFlume account at https://www.nvsilverflume.gov/
- **Current Platform:** ORION business portal at https://projectorion.nv.gov/ (launched December 9, 2025)
- **Single Sign-On:** Same username and password work for both SilverFlume and ORION
- **Future Transition:** SilverFlume scheduled to be discontinued in late summer 2026
- **Optional Trust Account:** Available for frequent filers with minimum $100 initial deposit (Contact Accounting Division at 775-684-5780)

**Payment Authorization:**
- Written authorization required to use trust account for filing fees
- Credit/debit card payments require: card number, expiration date, card issuer, cardholder name, and billing address

---

### 3. REQUIREMENTS
**Confidence Level:** HIGH (90%)

**For Filing:**
- Account registration on SilverFlume/ORION platform
- Use of prescribed forms only (filing officer will reject non-conforming forms)
- Written payment authorization for trust account or valid credit/debit card information
- Amendment filings require authorization from appropriate secured parties or debtors

**For Searching:**
- Free public searches: No account required for basic online searches
- Certified searches: Account required, fees apply

---

### 4. ACCESS METHOD
**Confidence Level:** HIGH (95%)

**Online Access:**
- **Primary Portal:** ORION Platform - https://projectorion.nv.gov/
- **Legacy Portal:** SilverFlume - https://www.nvsilverflume.gov/ (being phased out)
- **Availability:** 24/7 online filing and searching
- **Search Options:** 
  - By debtor name
  - By filing number
  - By secured party name

**Search Types:**
- **Free Public Searches:** Basic information access at no charge
- **Certified Searches:** Official certified search certificates available for fee (provides legally admissible documentation)

### 5. FEE SCHEDULE
**Confidence Level:** MEDIUM (70%)

Based on NRS 104.9525 and available documentation:

**Filing Fees (for records other than initial financing statements):**
- $60 - Written record, 1-2 pages
- $90 - Written record, 3-20 pages
- $2 - Each page over 20 pages
- $30 - Record communicated by electronic medium
- $2 - Each additional debtor, trade name, or business name reference

**Additional Fees:**
- $2 per page for copies or records produced at request
- Base filing fee for standard UCC-1: $40 (cited in third-party sources, not confirmed in statute)

**Search Fees:**
- Basic online searches: FREE
- Certified search certificates: Fee applies (specific amount not disclosed in search results)
- UCC-11 search requests: Fee applies (specific amount not disclosed in search results)

**Note:** The official fee schedule document on nvsos.gov was not accessible during research. For complete and current fee information, contact the Commercial Recordings Division directly at 775-684-5708 or visit https://www.nvsos.gov/sos/sos-information/forms-all-divisions/ucc-forms

### SUMMARY OF KEY FINDINGS

Nevada operates a modern, online UCC filing and search system through the new ORION platform, which replaced the legacy SilverFlume system in December 2025. The state follows a standard authorization model requiring account registration for filing, while offering free public searches for basic information and fee-based certified searches for legal documentation. The Nevada Secretary of State's Commercial Recordings Division serves as the central filing office under NRS 104.9501.
