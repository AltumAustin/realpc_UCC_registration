# UCC Searcher/Filing Access Requirements Research - Five States

## 1. NORTH CAROLINA

**Filing Office:** North Carolina Secretary of State - UCC Section

**Authorization Model:** **Open Access** - No formal authorized searcher program. Public access available to anyone.

**Key Fees:**
- **Online Filing:** $30 (UCC-1 or UCC-3) + $3 processing fee for credit card payment

## KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY

### Authorized Searcher Programs
**NONE of the five researched states (NC, ND, OH, OK, OR) have formal authorized searcher certification programs.** All five states provide open or public access to UCC searches:

- **Delaware** is the primary state known to require authorized searchers for certified searches
- The researched states all offer either free online public searches or fee-based certified searches available to anyone

### Access Models Breakdown
1. **Open Access (4 states):** NC, OH, OK, OR - Anyone can search and file
2. **Subscription-Based Portal (1 state):** ND - Requires login for certified searches, but public search available

### Filing Method Trends
- **Online Only:** North Dakota (paper no longer accepted)
- **Online Preferred:** Ohio (paper not accepted), Oregon (lower fees for online)
- **Online + Paper:** North Carolina, Oklahoma (both methods accepted)

### Fee Range
- **Lowest Filing Fee:** Ohio ($12)
- **Highest Filing Fee:** North Dakota ($40)
- **Free Searches:** All five states offer free online public searches
