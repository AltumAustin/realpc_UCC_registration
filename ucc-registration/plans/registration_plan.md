## Tiered Registration Approach

### Tier 1 — Immediate (Free Online Registration)

States where RealPC can register entirely online with no cost and minimal effort.

| State | Action | Portal | Est. Time |
|-------|--------|--------|-----------|
| **Alaska** | Create free filing account | dnr.alaska.gov/ssd/recoff/ucconline | Self-service |
| **California** | Create free bizfile Online account | bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov | Self-service |
| **Colorado** | Create free user account | sos.state.co.us/ucc/ | Self-service |
| **Connecticut** | Create free business.ct.gov account | business.ct.gov | Self-service |
| **Florida** | Create free filing account | floridaucc.com/OnlineFiling/Account/Register/ | Self-service |
| **Georgia** | Create free Limited-Use GSCCCA account | account.gsccca.org | Self-service |

**States with NO registration needed at all:**

| State | Status | Portal |
|-------|--------|--------|
| **Alabama** | Search available now (free portal, no account) | arc-sos.state.al.us |
| **Arizona** | Search available now (free portal, no account) | apps.azsos.gov/apps/ucc/search/ |

**Total Tier 1 cost: $0**

### Tier 2 — Near-Term (Paid Subscription / Simple Setup)

States requiring a paid subscription or account setup with modest fees.

| State | Action | Cost | Portal |
|-------|--------|------|--------|
| **Arkansas** | Register for INA subscriber account | $150/year | portal.arkansas.gov/ina-subscriber-account/ |
| **Alabama** (optional upgrade) | Register for Alabama Interactive subscriber | $95-120/year | alabamainteractive.org/subscriber_registration/ |
| **Georgia** (optional upgrade) | Upgrade to Regular GSCCCA account | $14.95/month | account.gsccca.org |

**Total Tier 2 cost: $150 - $450/year**

### Tier 3 — Complex (Requires Outreach / Formal Application)

States with extensive requirements or unclear processes.

| State | Issue | Action |
|-------|-------|--------|
| **Delaware** | Mandatory Authorized Searcher program; possibly closed | Contact Division of Corporations; explore partnership options |

**Total Tier 3 cost: Unknown pending outreach**

### Tier 4 — Clarification Needed

States where requirements are unclear and outreach is required before registration.

_No states currently in Tier 4 for the first 10. Delaware's core issue (Authorized Searcher) is well-understood; the clarification needed is whether the program accepts new applicants._

## Risk Factors

1. **Delaware** — Single largest risk. If the Authorized Searcher program is closed, RealPC will need to engage an existing authorized searcher as intermediary for Delaware UCC searches.

2. **Fee discrepancies** — Several states have conflicting fee information in online sources. All fees should be verified before committing to payment.

3. **Remaining 41 states** — Additional complex states (like Delaware) may be identified during continued research. States with known complexity include Mississippi (which uses a county-based system) and Louisiana (which has unique civil law implications for UCC).
